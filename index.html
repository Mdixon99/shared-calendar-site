Matt & Ally Shared Calendar

A personal SMS-based shared calendar

What It Does

This is a private shared calendar for two people. You text an event to the phone number, and it gets added to a shared Google Calendar. You can also query upcoming events, update existing ones, or cancel them — all via SMS.

How It Works

Send a text like "Dinner Friday 7pm at Nobu" and the system parses it, creates a Google Calendar event, and replies with a confirmation. You can ask "What's this week?" to see upcoming events.

Who Uses It

This is a personal service used by exactly two people — Matt Dixon and his partner Ally. There is no public sign-up. Both users are manually configured by the account owner.

Message Volume

Fewer than 50 messages per month. This is a personal tool, not a commercial product or marketing service.
