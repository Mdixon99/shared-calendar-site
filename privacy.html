Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 17, 2026

What Data We Collect

  • Phone numbers — The two authorized phone numbers are stored in the server configuration to identify users and route messages.
  • Message content — Incoming SMS messages are processed to extract calendar event details (title, date, time, location). Message content is logged for debugging purposes.
  • Calendar events — Events created through the Service are stored in a shared Google Calendar.

How Data Is Used

All data is used exclusively for the purpose of managing a shared calendar between two people. Specifically:

  • Phone numbers are used to authenticate users and send SMS replies.
  • Message content is parsed to determine the user's intent (create, query, update, or cancel an event).
  • Calendar data is stored in Google Calendar and accessible to both authorized users.

Data Sharing

Your data is never sold, rented, or shared with any third parties. The only external services involved are:

  • Twilio — for sending and receiving SMS messages (subject to Twilio's Privacy Policy).
  • Google Calendar API — for storing calendar events (subject to Google's Privacy Policy).

Data Storage

Server logs and configuration data are stored locally on a personal server operated by the account owner. No data is stored in cloud databases or third-party storage services beyond Google Calendar.

Data Retention

Server logs are retained for debugging purposes and may be periodically cleared. Calendar events persist in Google Calendar until deleted by a user.

Your Rights

As an authorized user, you may:

  • Request to see what data is stored about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of the Service at any time by replying STOP or contacting the operator.

Contact
