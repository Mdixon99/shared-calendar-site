Opt-In Disclosure

Last updated: April 17, 2026

How Users Opt In

Both authorized users provide verbal consent to the account owner (Matthew Dixon) before their phone numbers are added to the system. There is no online sign-up form, no web-based opt-in, and no automated enrollment process.

Who Can Use This Service

This Service is restricted to exactly two people — Matt Dixon and his partner Ally. Phone numbers are manually configured in the server by the account owner. No one else can send messages to or receive messages from this number.

What Users Consent To

  • Receiving SMS confirmations when calendar events are created, updated, or cancelled.
  • Receiving SMS notifications when the other user modifies a shared event.
  • Receiving SMS schedule summaries in response to queries.

Message Frequency

Message frequency depends on usage. Typical volume is fewer than 50 messages per month across both users combined.

How to Opt Out

  • Reply STOP to the Service phone number at any time to stop receiving messages.
  • Contact the account owner at matt.ally.calendar@gmail.com to have your number removed.
  • Opting out is immediate and permanent unless you request to be re-added.

No Public Enrollment

There is no mechanism for the public to sign up for this Service. Only the account owner can add or remove phone numbers. This is a personal tool, not a commercial messaging service.

Message & Data Rates

Standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply.

Contact

For questions about opting in or out, contact Matthew Dixon at matt.ally.calendar@gmail.com.
